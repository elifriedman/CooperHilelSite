Welcome to the Cooper Union Hillel

Cooper Union contains a small, but thriving Jewish community.

The mission of the Cooper Union Hillel is to engage the Jewish students of the Cooper Union and enrich their lives by:

  • providing religious and social experiences, including events corresponding to the Jewish holidays
  • providing Jewish education on campus
  • ensuring that religious needs (e.g. daily prayers, kosher food, . . . ) are met on campus
  • promoting the Jewish values of loving-kindness (chesed) and academic and social integrity within the culture of Cooper Union
